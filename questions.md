# The Marketing Reset - All Questions

## Concierge Questions (4 questions)

These are asked in the guided concierge flow before the full intake form. Answers pre-fill corresponding intake form fields.

### Q1: Where are you in your business right now? (maps to q11)

> There is no wrong answer. This helps me calibrate the strategy to your actual situation.

- Just starting out -- Building from scratch and figuring things out as you go
- Established but inconsistent -- Some weeks are great, others are quiet, and you are not sure why
- Fully booked -- You have the clients, but you want better-fit people or higher pricing
- Overwhelmed -- You are doing too much and something has to give
- Stagnant or plateaued -- Things are fine but you feel stuck and unsure what is next

### Q2: What matters most to you right now? (maps to q12)

> Pick the one thing that, if it changed, would make the biggest difference.

- More clients -- My schedule has too many open spots right now
- More consistent bookings -- I want steady work, not feast-or-famine
- Better-fit clients -- I want to work with people who value what I do
- Increase my pricing -- I know I am undercharging but I am not sure how to shift
- Reduce my workload -- I need to protect my energy and stop doing everything

### Q3: How would you describe your marketing right now? (maps to q21)

> Most service providers were never taught this. No judgment here.

- Word of mouth only -- Clients find me through referrals and I do not really market
- Occasional posting -- I show up on social media when I feel inspired or remember
- Regular but not working -- I am posting consistently but it does not seem to bring clients
- Referrals and repeats -- Almost all my business comes from people I already know
- Tried everything -- I have done courses, ads, content plans, and none of it stuck
- Do not know where to start -- I feel overwhelmed and I just need someone to tell me what to focus on

### Q4: What is the biggest thing standing in your way? (maps to q39)

> Knowing your real constraint helps me build a strategy that actually fits your life.

- Time -- I am stretched thin as it is
- Money -- I can not invest much right now
- Confidence -- I do not trust my own marketing instincts
- Consistency -- I start things but never stick with them
- Clarity -- I do not even know what my brand should be
- Burnout -- I am running on empty
- Direction -- I genuinely do not know what works and what does not
